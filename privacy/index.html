Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 2024

This Privacy Policy describes how Selrad App ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares your personal information when you use our application ("Планировщик").

1. Data Collection

We collect and process the following types of personal and confidential data:

  • Work Information: This includes todo lists, tasks completed, and related work metrics.
  • Geolocation Data: For drivers who perform deliveries, we collect and store geolocation data during work hours.

This data is collected to compute performance metrics, improve services, calculate salaries, and enhance overall application functionality.

2. Data Usage

The data we collect is used for the following purposes:

  • Performance analytics and metrics
  • Service improvement
  • Salary calculations

3. Data Retention and Deletion

Your work and geolocation data are stored for no longer than 30 days after the completion of the related tasks. After this period, all task-related data, including geolocation information, is permanently deleted from our systems.

4. Geolocation Data Usage

Geolocation data is only collected and accessed during the working hours of employees who are responsible for deliveries. This information is used solely for work-related purposes and performance evaluation.

5. Data Sharing

Your data is not shared with any third-party companies, except as required by law or to protect the rights, property, or safety of our company, our users, or others.

6. Data Security

We take appropriate measures to ensure the confidentiality and security of your data, including the use of encryption and secure storage solutions.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. To submit a request or if you have any questions about this policy, please contact our privacy officer.

8. Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to make a request, you can reach us at:

Email: info@selrad.kz

9. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be effective immediately upon posting the updated policy in the App and on our website.

Thank you for using our app.
